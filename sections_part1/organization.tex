\projacro\ is a PRME project that will be hosted at Aix-Marseille Université (amU), 
particularly at the Centre de Physique des Particules de Marseille (CPPM). 
The team consists of researchers already active in the field, all members of either 
DESI and ZTF collaborations, spaning a wide range of expertise.

\subsection{Implemented and requested resources to reach the objectives}

For the success of \projacro, we would like to request the funding of
one doctoral researcher (137~977,00~€) and one experienced postdoctoral researcher (253~378,00~€), 
each for the duration of 36 months. 
Each position will be assisted with funds for a work computer (2k~€ each)
and travel support (2.5k~€ per year per person and 1k~€ per year for the scientific coordinator).
Our results will be published in the peer-reviewed journal Astronomy and Astrophysics, 
and we request 3k~€ for eventual associated publication fees. 
Including fees, \textbf{the total budget of \projacro\ amounts to 473 697,93~€ for a duration of 48 months.}
